About us


CN COMPUTERS Commerce Group Co., Ltd. principally operates franchised retail shops of electronics appliances in China. The operation categories include Laptops & Computers, Tablets & E-Readers, Video Games, Consoles & VR. Founded by CY and MYM, CN COMPUTERS will issue its initial public offering of stock on May 15, 2028 trading under the NASDAQ stock exchange symbol CNC.
